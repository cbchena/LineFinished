LineFinished Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026 · Last updated: June 13, 2026

Welcome to LineFinished ("the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle information related to you.

In short: LineFinished is a fully offline puzzle game. We do not collect or upload your personal identity information, and no account is required.

1. Information We Collect

We do not actively collect, store, or transmit your personally identifiable information (such as name, phone number, email, or precise location) to any server.

The App does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or user-tracking SDKs.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide game features, the following data is stored only on your device (via iOS local storage):

  • Level progress, star ratings, and best times
  • Daily challenge, Endless mode, and Advanced mode records
  • Language preference settings

This data is not synced to the cloud and is not sent to us or any third party. Uninstalling the App deletes this local data.

3. Photo Library Permission

When you tap "Save Share Image," the App requests permission to add photos only to your library, so share posters can be saved to your Photos app.

You may deny or revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos. Denying access does not affect core gameplay.

4. Network and Accounts

Core gameplay works without an internet connection. No account creation is required to access all game content.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we do not collect personal identity information, no personal data is submitted to us on a server when anyone uses the App.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version is available in the App under Settings → Privacy Policy. For material changes, we will provide notice within the App where appropriate.
